Project Mosaic

Project Mosaic is a youth-led initiative committed to supporting overlooked and underserved communities—including immigrants, families affected by poverty, unhoused individuals, and neglected animals—through hands-on action, community connection, and monthly supply drives.

Founded in Lynwood, California, Project Mosaic was created by students with lived experience and a passion for tangible change. We believe every piece matters. Just like a mosaic is built from many unique fragments, strong communities are built when people come together with care, dignity, and purpose.

Our Impact So Far

  • 8+ bulk donations collected (food, clothing, baby items, shoes, animal supplies)
  • 10+ active youth volunteers across multiple states
  • 15,000+ digital reach and growing community awareness
  • Partnerships with Hands of Love Center and HOPICS
  • Monthly community donation drives and weekly outreach canvassing
  • Mentorship and backing through the PIVOT entrepreneurial network

Project Mosaic began as a small, student-led effort that has grown into a multi-state youth movement focused on delivering real resources to communities that need them most—including immigrant families navigating periods of instability.
