Community Resource Hub

This Community Resource Hub connects immigrants and underserved families in the United States with trusted, verified support. The organizations below provide legal assistance, know-your-rights education, housing help, food access, health services, and emergency support.

Every resource listed here is from a reputable nonprofit or government source. This hub is designed to reduce confusion, save time, and help individuals and families find real help quickly and safely. Resources will continue to expand as more verified support organizations are added.

Note: Availability of services may vary by location and immigration status. Always contact organizations directly to confirm eligibility and current services.
